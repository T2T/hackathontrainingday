About this Hackathon + Training Day

This hackathon is brought to you by Tech LadyMafia, Rails Girls, PyLadies, Data Lady Alliance, Code for DC, and more! Coders, tech strategists, trainers, newbies, and ideas are welcome!

The goal of this hackathon + training day is for you to contribute to a project you find meaningful, learn new tech, make new friends and connections, and then attend more DC tech meetups/hackathons - especially Code for DC! We want you to walk away feeling inspired and empowered to get more involved in tech. We're still working out the details. Please share your feedback in the form or through one of the links below.

Training
We're going to offer 101s throughout the day. Attend training all day, or dip in and out while you contribute to a hackathon project. Right now, we're thinking of offering Python, Ruby, SQL, GitHub, APIs, Open Data, and Google Analytics. Are we missing anything important?

Hackathon
We're planning to work on some projects and challenges suggested by lady small business and organizations that use gov open data, including some civic projects. Please submit ideas.
